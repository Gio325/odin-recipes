Pizza Margherita

Description

The Margherita pizza is the typical Neapolitan pizza. Topped with tomato, mozzarella, fresh basil, salt, and oil, it is, along with the Marinara pizza, the most popular Italian pizza.

It is undoubtedly the ultimate symbol of Italy's cultural and culinary heritage, renowned and enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients for 1 pizza

  • 100 g Tomato (canned peeled tomatoes or tomato sauce)
  • 100 g Mozzarella (Fior di Latte or Buffalo Mozzarella)
  • 3-4 leaves Fresh basil
  • A pinch of Salt
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil (extra virgin)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough (4-6 hours before baking)
    1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in lukewarm water. Add the sugar (if using) and mix. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
    2. In a large bowl, add the flour and mix in the salt.
    3. Slowly pour in the yeast mixture, mixing with a spoon or your hand.
    4. Add olive oil and knead for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    5. Form a ball, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 4-6 hours, or until doubled in size.

  2. Prepare the tomato sauce
